Future Classic Movies

May 18, 2021 - 1 minutes read - 162 words

The Past

  • Limited number of movies released
  • With the advent of the VCR, you could own a movie
    • Continued with DVD and Blue-ray

The Present

  • Streaming is becoming the go to place especially during a pandemic
  • Streaming quality is getting better along with internet speeds
  • Streaming services are making their own content
  • Streaming services is a rental model and not a purchase
  • Streaming services don’t make physical versions of their content
    • The internet is not fast everywhere

The Future

  • Streaming services can stop showing certain content
    • e.g. Nick Offerman’s stand-up (find link)
  • Without physical versions, there is no amount of money that can purchase content
  • It will be difficult to show the next generation movies that are not “worth” keeping on a streaming service

Is there a fix?

  • This practice will encourage pirating content
    • Possible to watch even when pulled from the streaming service
  • Old physical media could be revitalized when found in a basement
    • What analog is there for streaming content?
